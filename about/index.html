Hey, I'm Sen! I love tinkering and schminkering. I enjoy reverse engineering, patching, gaming, and getting lost in all kinds of awesome media.

I tend to stay pretty low-profile online, but I really enjoy talking to people who share the same interests. You can usually find me in the NFSCO community, where I also manage NFSCO Crew Battles, an unofficial branch that hosts crew battles in the style of classic Need for Speed: Carbon.

At home I live with two little troublemakers, my cat Lemon and my tortoise Taki. I'm a big Soulslike fan and have platinumed most of the FromSoft games. I also love metal, especially nu metal and rock bands like Linkin Park, Mudvayne, and Element Eighty.

My favorite film genre is psychological horror, which probably explains why my favorite anime include Serial Experiments Lain, Death Note, Neon Genesis Evangelion, Haibane Renmei, and Elfen Lied.

I'm also pretty proud of my PC setup. It runs a Ryzen 7 9700X and a Radeon 7800 XT in a Lian Li A3 case, with a 27” 1440p OLED as my main screen and a 21.5” 1080p as my secondary. I use Edifier R1280DB speakers because I really hate wearing headphones. I also love collecting controllers and consoles: I have a few PS2 Slims, a PS2 Fat, a PS3 Slim, a PS4, a 2DS, a SNES edition 3DS XL, a PSP Street, a couple of PSP 2000s, and way too many others to list.

Thanks for stopping by, and feel free to join The Rabbithole if you want to chat!
